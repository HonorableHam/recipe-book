Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast (4)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Condensed cream of chicken soup. (2 cans)
  • Finely Diced Onion (1)
  • Biscut dough (20oz)

Instructions

  1. Place the biscut dough in the refrigerator, and the rest of the ingredients in the slow cooker.
  2. Cover the ingredients in the cooker with water
  3. Set your cooker on High, and act productive for 5 to 6 hours.
  4. 30 minutes before serving, take the biscut dough out of the refrigerator, tear it into bite sized pieces, and add it to the cooker.
