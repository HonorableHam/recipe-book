Black Eyed Peas

Ingredients

  • Water (6 Cups)
  • Chicken Bouillon (1 Cube)
  • Dried Black Eyed Peas (1 Pound)
  • Onion (1, Diced)
  • Garlic (2 Cloves, Miced)
  • Red Bell Pepper (1, Steamed and Diced)
  • Jalapeno (1, Minced)
  • Ham (8oz, Diced)
  • Thick Cut Bacon (4 Slices, Chopped)
  • Cayenne Pepper (1 Teaspoon)
  • Cumin (1 1/2 Teaspoons)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Add water and bouillon to slow cooker and stir until dissolved.
  2. Add solid ingredients to slow cooker.
  3. Turn slow cooker on low and cover.
  4. Go do something else for 6 to 8 hours. It's called a slow cooker for a reason.
